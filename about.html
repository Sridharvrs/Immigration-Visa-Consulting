🏢 Our Story

From a small consulting idea to a global immigration support network.

How It All Started

Stackly was founded with a simple mission — to make immigration guidance transparent, reliable, and accessible for everyone. What started as a small advisory service has now grown into a trusted global consultancy helping thousands achieve their dreams abroad.

Our Growth Journey

Over the years, we expanded our services across student visas, work permits, PR applications, and business migration. Today, we proudly support clients in 20+ countries with expert-driven solutions and high success rates.
